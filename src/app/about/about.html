About Us

We offer paper floral backdrops and decorations for any party, wedding, or special occasion. From traditional to modern, Dazzling Deco provides unique and customizable styles to meet your needs. You can choose from our selections, or turn your vision into a reality with our custom option. We understand how important your special occasion is and promise our best quality service to exceed your expectations.

Dazzling Deco is a family owned company. Jane Kotzalov, owner, has a vision of beautiful backgrounds and decorations. While in nursing school, she develops ideas and manages the business. Roumen Kotzalov, her dad, is the architect and set-up manager. He hand-crafts paper flowers, builds panels, and carves letters. Nasi Kotzalov, her mom, is the public relations point of contact. She spreads the word and gets our name out. Together, this trio is dazzling the Central Florida area with unique backdrops and decorative creations!
